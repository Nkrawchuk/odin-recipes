Cereal sandwich

This is a recipe for a bowl of cereal. I think it's clear that I've lost a lot of steam on these.

One of my breakfasts. Hope you enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Corn flakes (or whatever)

Steps

  1. Pour cereal from box into bowl. Very important that this comes first.
  2. Next, pour the milk into your cereal until all but the top layer of cereal have been coated.

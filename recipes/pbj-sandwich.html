Peanut butter & jelly sandwich

This is a recipe for the best sandwich. The peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

It's very easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take 2 slices of bread, lay flat.
  2. Using a knife, scoop as much peanut butter as you possibly can and spread evenly on one slice of bread.
  3. Using a spoon, grab some of that jelly and spread that evenly on the other slice of bread.
  4. Using your hands, slap those two pieces of bread together.
